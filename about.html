Who We Are

We conduct all kinds of activities to get safe telehealth and telemedicine at home.

The project aims to support the 100 Million Health Initiative by using artificial intelligence to identify diabetes, pressure, and anemia diseases through the image of the patient's eye and retina. and It provides support to doctors and reduces overcrowding and the spread of diseases. It also provides health instructions for the user, and a robot answers the patient’s questions, recommends the patient to the nearest source of health care to him (a doctor, pharmacy, or analysis laboratory).

In addition, there will be a bot that answers all the patient’s questions related to his disease, and finally, the patient will be able to upload his analyzes and medical history, where our website will be It is a patient's personal health account.
